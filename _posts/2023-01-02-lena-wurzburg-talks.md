title: Two Talks in Würzburg in January 2023 by Lena Henningsen
---

To kickstart the new year in a readerly fashion, Lena Henningsen will deliver two talks in early January at the University of Würzburg.

<span class="image left"><img src="{% link assets/images/LenaWurzburg1.jpg %}" alt="" /></span>

First, on January 9 starting at 19:15, READCHINA's PI will talk with Roland Altenburger at a lecture series covering various winners of the Nobel Prize in Literature about "Gao Xingjian und Mo Yan: Die chinesischen Literaturnobelpreise zwischen Avantgarde, Anpassung und Anklage." For details, see [here](https://www.uni-wuerzburg.de/aktuelles/einblick/single/news/ringvorlesung-der-literaturnobelpreis/).

<span class="image right"><img src="{% link assets/images/LenaWurzburg2.jpg %}" alt="" /></span>

Then, on January 10 starting at 18:00, Lena Henningsen will present her most recent research in a talk titled "Reading in Sequence: Readerly qualities of Chinese comic adaptations from Lu Xun to Lei Feng." Details [here](https://www.uni-wuerzburg.de/forschung/china-centre/aktuelles/single/news/vortrag-reading-in-sequence-readerly-qualities-of-chinese-comic-adaptations-from-lu-xun-to-lei-feng/).

We kindly invite all READCHINA friends and colleagues who are in the area to join the talks in person!
